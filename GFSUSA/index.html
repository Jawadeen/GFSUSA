About GFSUSA

Our mission

GFSUSA exists to unite Ghanaians in South Africa around organised, disciplined support for Ghanaian football teams during their international games and travels.

Who we are

Ghana Football Supporters Union South Africa (GFSUSA) is a body formed to bring Ghanaians together in support of our national and club teams whenever they travel outside Ghana, especially when they play in South Africa.

  • We coordinate match-day presence, songs and support in the stands.
  • We promote unity, discipline and pride among Ghanaian supporters abroad.
  • We align with recognised structures where possible to support Ghana officially.

What we focus on

  • Supporting Ghanaian teams playing in South Africa.
  • Sending delegations to key fixtures outside both Ghana and South Africa where possible.
  • Helping members understand correct documentation and travel processes.
  • Building a strong, visible Ghanaian community around football.
What we do

Core activities

From singing in the stands to representing supporters at meetings, GFSUSA’s work centres on practical, visible support for Ghanaian teams.

Match support

  • Mobilising supporters when Ghanaian teams play in South Africa.
  • Organising drums, songs, flags and colours for the stands.
  • Creating a home atmosphere for Ghana, even away from Ghana.

Delegations & community

  • Sending GFSUSA delegations to selected fixtures outside Ghana and South Africa where feasible.
  • Engaging the Ghanaian community through meetings and updates.
  • Encouraging proper documentation and responsible representation of Ghana.
Leadership

Our executives

GFSUSA operates with an executive structure and a four-year cycle for leadership and membership.

The Union consists of executives, a Chairman, an Organizer, a Secretary, an Administrator, Directors and general members. Members contribute monthly dues to sustain activities, and leadership elections are held every four years.

Chairman & Executives

Provide overall direction and represent GFSUSA in engagements with partners, supporters and institutions.

Secretary & Administrator

Manage correspondence, records and internal communication, ensuring the Union’s work is well documented.

Organizer & Directors

Lead mobilisation, match-day logistics and different portfolios such as welfare, media and supporter coordination.

Membership

Join GFSUSA

Membership is open to Ghanaians and Ghanaian supporters in South Africa who share the aim of supporting Ghanaian football teams.

Membership outline

Members align with the mission of GFSUSA and are willing to participate in supporter activities and contribute monthly dues to sustain the Union’s work.

  • Monthly member dues help fund logistics, mobilisation and communication.
  • Members without proper documentation are encouraged to obtain the correct papers in Ghana; those with sufficient documentation may receive support through the Union where appropriate.
  • Active members may take part in meetings, events and, where applicable, leadership roles.

How to express interest

To express interest in membership or to request more information, please contact GFSUSA using the details below. A member of the leadership team will follow up.

  • Share your name and where in South Africa you are based.
  • Indicate that you would like to become a member of GFSUSA.
  • Mention any previous supporter or organisational experience, if applicable.

Contact & Engagement

Work with GFSUSA

GFSUSA is open to engagement with Ghanaian institutions, football bodies and stakeholders who share the goal of supporting Ghanaian teams abroad.

Collaboration & meetings

GFSUSA is available to meet with relevant bodies, including the Ghana High Commission in South Africa and the National Supporters Union (NSU), to discuss how we can work together in coordinating support for Ghanaian teams.

Our intention is to align our activities with recognised structures, enhance representation, and ensure that Ghana is supported in a unified and organised way whenever teams travel.
